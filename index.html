    Description:

    Eggy Car is an exciting physics-based driving game that puts your balance and control skills to the test. Your mission? Transport a delicate egg across challenging, hilly terrains without breaking it. With simple controls but demanding gameplay, each level presents a unique challenge that will keep you coming back for more.

    How To Play

    The concept is simple but mastering it takes skill. Drive your car across various terrain while keeping the egg safe in your vehicle. Balance is key - too much speed or a rough landing could spell disaster for your precious cargo. Progress through increasingly challenging levels, each requiring more precise control and strategic thinking.

    Controls

    • Right Arrow or D: Accelerate forward
    • Left Arrow or A: Accelerate backward

    Tips and Tricks

    • Master the Speed: Control your velocity - speed isn't always the answer!
    • Watch the Egg: Keep an eye on how the egg moves in your vehicle.
    • Smooth Landings: Try to land as flat as possible to prevent the egg from bouncing.
    • Practice Makes Perfect: Don't get discouraged if you fail - each attempt teaches you something new.
    • Momentum Management: Use the hills to your advantage by managing your momentum carefully.
