    Forget the gritty realism and serious storylines of other shooters. Funny Shooter 2 is here to remind us that sometimes, all we need is a good dose of humor to make a game truly enjoyable. This sequel to the hit indie title takes everything that made the original hilarious and cranks it up to eleven.
